How to use

Tap the chapter button to open the chapter list and jump to any section. Tap a Pali line to view its English translation. Use the minus and plus buttons to adjust the text size for easier reading.

Acknowledgment

The main text in this app is based primarily on the printed chanting booklet used by the compiler, with additional reference to A Little Book of Buddhist Chanting. English translations were prepared with assistance from GPT and Gemini, then reviewed and adapted for this edition.
