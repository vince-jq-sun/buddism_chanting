How to use

iPhone users: for a cleaner full-screen reading experience, open this site in Safari, tap Share, and choose Add to Home Screen. Then open it from the Home Screen icon to reduce browser chrome while reading.

Tap the lower-left three-line button to open the chapter list and jump to any section. Tap the pli or en button to switch the main text language; lines without translation stay unchanged. Tap a line to view the other language. Use the minus and plus buttons to adjust the text size.
